Nine knit designers have come together to create this project and share it with you. We each started with a basic sleeve and body pattern, then designed a unique yoke for your knitting pleasure!

Pick your favourite, or make a few; each versatile design is sized from baby to big for knits for the whole family.

What is the same about these designs? They are all knit from the bottom up (you start with sleeves, then knit the body, then join it all to work the yoke). They are all knit in DK weight yarn, at the same gauge; so you can use the same yarn for any of the designs. Each design includes the same extensive range of sizes,

This pattern is one of 8 designs included in the Heart On My Sleeve ebook, a charity project in support of the Against Malaria Foundation!

Buy it now for $18.

The Heart On My Sleeve ebook will be available for purchase until Feb 28 2018. All sale incomes (purchase price minus Paypal and Ravelry fees) from this ebook will be donated to the Against Malaria Foundation. For full details of the funds raised to date, and to donate directly, visit: https://www.againstmalaria.com/heartonmysleeveknits

As you can see, each design is sized from baby to big; so you can knit for grandbaby or for grandma!
