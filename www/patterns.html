Nine knit designers have come together to create this project and share it with you. We each started with a basic sleeve and body pattern, then designed a unique yoke for your knitting pleasure!

Pick your favourite, or make a few; each versatile design is sized from baby to big for knits for the whole family.

What is the same about these designs? They are all knit from the bottom up (you start with sleeves, then knit the body, then join it all to work the yoke). They are all knit in DK weight yarn, at the same gauge; so you can use the same yarn for any of the designs.

And as you can see, each design is sized from baby to big; so you can knit for grandbaby or for grandma!

The Heart On My Sleeve ebook, is a charity project in support of the Against Malaria Foundation. It includes 8 sweater designs by nine designers.
