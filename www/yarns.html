Many wonderful yarn companies and dyers offered their wholehearted support of this project! For the Heart On My Sleeve sweaters we used a wide range of yarns and fibres, all in a DK weight.

We can recommend the lovely yarns we have used, but we also encourage you to support yarn producers and dyers local to you. Because the yarns used in these designs are all DK weight, and all the patterns are designed for the same gauge, you can simply use your favourite locally available DK weight yarn and it will work for any of the designs in the ebook. If you are uncertain, visit your local yarn shop where the friendly staff can help you choose the right yarn for your sweater.

Thanks again to the supportive businesses who provided yarn for this project, and prizes for the knitalong.
